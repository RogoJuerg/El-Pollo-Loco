El Pollo Loco

Walk = Left/Right
Jump = Spacebar
Throw Bottle = D
Buy Bottle = F

Our hero called "El Pollo Loco" has to defeat the evil giant chicken to rescue his village. Tequilla is not cheap so you have to buy it at your local shop. Be careful! That giant chicken likes to throw his minions at his enemies! And do not try to jump on a chicken to defeat it... This is not a video game!
